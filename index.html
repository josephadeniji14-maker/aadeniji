Who I Am

A dual-discipline technical professional holding a Higher National Diploma in Electrical Engineering, currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with an anticipated completion in 2027. By day, I service and commission multi-million dollar fire trucks for industrial and emergency response clients across Nigeria. Outside of that, I build software.

I work at John Holt Plc, Nigeria's only authorised Rosenbauer partner, where I am the face of the Fire and Safety Solutions department. I am currently undergoing online OEM training with Rosenbauer — the first phase toward full product specialist certification. For my clients, a fire truck that does not start is not a technical failure. It is a life safety failure. That weight is not lost on me.

On the software side, I work with Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AI APIs, and cloud tools — self-taught and course-supported. This portfolio itself is one example. The goal is not to choose between engineering and software. It is to be the rare professional who does not have to.
